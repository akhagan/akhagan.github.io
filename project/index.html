    • Design webpages for DiversifyMicrobiology & DiversifyImmunology

  • Investigator, Department Invited Speaker Diversity, November 2018 - Present.

    • Collected and analyzed demographics of previously invited speakers for trends in diversity.

    • Presented at department faculty meeting.

  • Instructor, Software Carpentry, Present.

    • Teach coding to novice learners in a Women in Science and Engineering workshop.

  • Participant, Future of Research Mentoring Symposium, UM Satellite, June 2019.

    • Advocating for mentorship accountability.

  • Planner, Department Retreat DEI Session, October 2018.

  • Instructor, Ethiopia-Michigan Platform for Advancing Collaborative Engagements, 2014 - 2015.

    • Prepared and taught curriculum for immunology and renal microbiology at a medical school in Ethiopia.

Diversify Digest
The goal for Diversify is to provide resources for scientists who are learning and growing in their anti-racism. The Digest is a biweekly curation of the latest research on diversity, equity, inclusion, and justice.

Diversify Immunology
The DiversifyImmunology site aims to identify Ph.D.s in Immunology who are women and/or from a group traditionally underrepresented or underserved in the sciences (e.g., those from racial or ethnic minorities, those with disabilities, people who identify as LGBTQIA). Our goal is to help symposium organizers, award committees, search committees, etc. identify immunologists who might diversify their pool.
